Cabbage, carrots, onions, parsnips, celery...
4 tablespoons thyme
2 tablespoons pickling spice
1 corned beef b (3 pounds)

In a slow cooker, place vegetables (except for cabbage). Place corned beef, fat side up. Sprinkle with pickling spice. Add liquid—half water, half Guinness—to cover meat.

Cover and cook on low for 8 hours.

Add cabbagem, recover, and cook for an additional 2 hours.

Thinly slice corned beef against the grain, and serve with mustard and creamy horseradish sauce.

Pepper may be needed.

Extra salt is NOT needed, or recommended
